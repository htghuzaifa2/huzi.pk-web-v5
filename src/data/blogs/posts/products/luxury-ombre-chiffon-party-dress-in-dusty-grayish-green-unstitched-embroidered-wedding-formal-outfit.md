## Styling Tips & How to Wear It

The beauty of chiffon lies in its movement—let the dress flow naturally. Pin the dupatta at the shoulder for security while maintaining that effortless drape that makes chiffon so captivating.

Remember, the best outfit is one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. This piece is designed to do exactly that, whether you're wearing it for party / formal events or simply stepping out in style.

## Care Instructions for Long-Lasting Beauty

Hand wash gently in cold water with a mild detergent. Do not twist or wring. Hang to dry in shade—direct sunlight can fade the colors. Iron on the lowest setting with a cloth barrier.

Proper care ensures that your investment continues to look as stunning as the day you received it. Taking these simple steps will keep your chiffon piece in pristine condition for years to come.
