## The Psychology of the "WFH Uniform"

Why do we care about a hoodie when we aren't even leaving the house? Because of **Enclothed Cognition.** This is the scientifically proven idea that what you wear affects how you think and perform.

If you stay in your wrinkled pajamas all day, your brain stays in "sleep mode." You're more likely to procrastinate, feel sluggish, and take longer to get into deep work. But if you put on a high-quality, comfortable hoodie, you flip a switch. You're cozy, but you're *ready.* The physical act of changing into "work clothes" — even if those clothes are just a nice hoodie — signals to your brain that it's time to focus.

In the Pakistani context, where the line between "home" and "office" is blurred by unexpected guests, family demands, and the constant temptation of the bed, having a "uniform" that looks good for a last-minute Zoom call is a survival strategy. You can't predict when your boss will say "Let's jump on a quick video call," but you can always be ready.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the difference between Kimi.pk and Kimi.com.pk?

Total confusion, right? **Kimi.pk** is a gadget and tech store (think keyboards, chargers, smartwatches, cables). **Kimi.com.pk** is a fashion and apparel brand (think hoodies, t-shirts, streetwear). They are two completely different companies with two different vibes, but both are leading the "niche e-commerce" wave in Pakistan. Don't mix them up when searching!

### Does the keyboard lag during gaming?

It's a Bluetooth keyboard, so it has a slight delay (~20ms) compared to a wired keyboard. It's perfect for RPG/strategy games, coding, writing, and general productivity. But I wouldn't recommend it for competitive CS:GO or Valorant, where every millisecond matters. For that, you need a wired or 2.4GHz wireless gaming keyboard.

### How do I wash the hoodie without ruining the print?

Turn it **inside out**, wash in cold water (never hot!), and never, ever put it in the dryer — let it air dry. If you must iron it, don't touch the print directly with the iron — put a cloth over it. This is how you make a Rs. 3,600 hoodie last for 3+ years. Also, avoid using bleach or strong detergents.

### Is Kimi.pk's shipping fast?

In my experience, within Lahore, I got the keyboard in 24 hours. For Karachi/Islamabad, it usually takes 2-3 days. They use reliable couriers (TCS, Leopards), so you aren't hunting for your package at a random warehouse in Gujranwala. They also provide tracking numbers.

### Can I connect the keyboard to more than 3 devices?

The keyboard has 3 dedicated Bluetooth channels (switched via the E1/E2/E3 buttons). If you need to connect to a 4th device, you'll need to unpair one of the existing connections first. For most people, 3 simultaneous connections is more than enough.
