## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Can AI do my whole assignment?

Technically, yes. Practically, no. University checkers (Turnitin, AI-detectors) are increasingly sophisticated. Use AI to **explain** concepts and **skeleton** code, but write the final version yourself. Use AI as a tutor, not a ghostwriter—if you can't explain the code the AI wrote, you'll fail the viva anyway.

### Which model is best for Urdu?

Both **DeepSeek** and **Z-AI** handle Urdu excellently—both Urdu script and Roman Urdu. They understand Pakistani context in ways Western AI tools simply cannot.

### What about local/open-source models?

In 2026, models like **Llama 3.1 70B**, **Mistral Large**, and **Qwen 2.5** can run locally on a decent laptop using Ollama. They're free, private, and work offline—perfect for load-shedding. If you have a laptop with 16GB RAM and a decent GPU, running a local coding model gives you assistance even when your ISP takes the day off.

The uniquely Pakistani problem. First, use a laptop with 6+ hours battery life. Second, keep Ollama with a local model as offline fallback. Third, use a power bank for your 4G hotspot. Local model + mobile hotspot means you're never completely without AI assistance, even during 4-hour blackouts.
