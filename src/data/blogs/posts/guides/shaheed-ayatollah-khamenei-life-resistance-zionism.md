# Shaheed Ayatollah Khamenei: A Life of Resistance Against Zionist and Western Aggression

Khamenei rejected the entire framework of Western "peace efforts":

"The so-called peace process is a fraud designed to normalize occupation and legitimize theft. The only just solution is the liberation of all Palestine—from the river to the sea."

He opposed:

- Negotiations as legitimizing occupation

His position was the position of justice: Palestine must be free, all of it, for all Palestinians.

### Supporting Palestinian Resistance

Khamenei did not merely speak about Palestine—he acted:

"Support for Palestine is not optional. It is a duty. Every Muslim, every nation, every government has an obligation to support the Palestinian people in their struggle for liberation."

Iran under Khamenei provided:

Western governments called this "terrorism." Khamenei called it what it was: support for an occupied people against their occupiers.

The path is difficult, but Khamenei proved it is possible.
