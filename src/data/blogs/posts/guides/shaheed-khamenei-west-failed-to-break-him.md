## The Assassination Attempts

A tape recorder had been placed beside the podium, packed with explosives. When it detonated, it:
- Severely damaged his right arm
- Injured his vocal cords
- Nearly killed him

He survived, but his right arm was permanently disabled. The physical wound healed, but the scar remained—a permanent reminder of the cost of resistance.
