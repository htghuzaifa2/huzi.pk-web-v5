## Britain's Criminal Role: The Balfour Declaration

### A Promise to Give Away What Wasn't Theirs

In 1917, while Palestine was still under Ottoman rule, British Foreign Secretary Arthur Balfour issued a declaration promising to support "the establishment in Palestine of a national home for the Jewish people." This promise was made to European Zionists without consulting the Palestinians who constituted 90% of the population.

Imagine if China promised California to Mexican immigrants without consulting Americans. That is the absurdity and criminality of the Balfour Declaration—a colonial power promising land it did not own to people who did not live there.
